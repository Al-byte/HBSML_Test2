Ep5 - would you pick hard work over talent?

Ep4 - This is why I chose basketball over football

Tune in as this very special guest dives deep into the reason he chose basketball over football, even though he had the likes of USC offering him!

Ep3 - How Did He Overcome that?

Mike Neal has a story to tell, and you won't want to miss it! He had a major surgery in his upper extremities (tune in to find out where), then his JuCo coach gets fired. The new coach comes in and releases him while he is recovering from brain surgery...oops! I let it slip. He then goes on to walk-on somewhere else, goes on to play NCAA D1, and play overseas! Tune in!

Ep2 - What An Underdog Story!

Kellen McCoy opens up about coaching, and not being a sideline coach at his own daughters games. He goes on to explain how they won the EYBL Peach Jam Circuit. Kellen goes into major detail about his playing days --playing with Damian Lillard-- getting cut overseas, and his road to redemption before he willingly walked away from playing for a life of coaching...tune in!

Ep1 - These Coaches Deserve A Shout Out

In this episode we pay a tribute to Kobe Bryant, we shout out some of the most influential coaches in our lives, and we highlight some of the qualities that make great coaches.
